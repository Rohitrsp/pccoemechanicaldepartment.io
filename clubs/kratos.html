Team Kratos Racing

About us

Team Kratos Racing where students from second, third and final years from various engineering disciplines come together for a common aim of learning and applying engineering tought in day to day life. The highly dedicated team of talented engineering graduates as vision to build an engineered, safe, light and reliable formula student car.

Objective:
SUPRA, FORMULA BHARAT and GO KART are National Level Engineering Student Competitions where Teams from Various Engineering Colleges Participate. In this competition Students Design and Fabricate a Vehicle. The students not only design the vehicle but also built them as per the given set of rules (Rule Book) and design standards of SAE International. Making the students learn and realize their talent through such an experience, objective of this event is to extract the future Entrepreneurs, Technocrats, Innovators and Leaders , thus serving the prime objective of bringing out the real talent to serve the Indian Automotive Industry for the years to come, in continuation with the vision Automotive Mission Plan, a dream to make India an International Automotive hub.
Outcome:

1. Students will be able to learn skills like designing, fabrication, cost management, and marketing.

2. Students will be able to develop good communication and presentation skills.

3. Students will be able to inherit self learning.

4. Students will be able to work in a team.

Faculty Co-ordinators

Prof. Amol Suryavanshi has completed M. Tech Automobile from VJTI Mumbai and Prof. Nilesh Gaikwad has completed M. Tech Heat Power Engineering from VNIT Nagpur. Both are having working experience more than 5 years and taught Automobile Engineering to UG students. They are guiding as faculty advisors to Team Kratos Racing right from inception.

Under their able guidance, team has managed to stand as National champion in SAE SUPRA competition consecutively in year 2016 and 2017. For their dedicated involvement and support to team, they were conferred with Dronacharya Award for best faculty Advisors in SUPRA 2016.

FORMULA BHARAT 2017

Team Kratos Racing participated in FORMULA BHARAT 2017 event which was held at Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore between 25th to 29th Jan 2017.

This is the first time we have participated in First Edition of Formula Bharat event which was earlier named as Formula Student India.
