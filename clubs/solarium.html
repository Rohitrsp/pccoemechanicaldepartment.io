Team Solarium

About us

We are a team of dedicated engineering students working towards a greener tomorrow. We build automobiles that do not require burning of fossil fuels for their propulsion, but, a very abundantly available, renewable type of energy source, THE SUN. We came into existence, as a team in the month of September 2016, and went on to design a solar-electric vehicle within 50 days, built the working prototype in 90 days and had a successful competition in March 2017.

Invictus v1.0: A competition, race-ready vehicle capable of carrying the driver that runs on the power from the sun or the charge stored in its batteries. It is capable of a top speed of 45 kmph by using just a 2hp motor. It was designed and built to compete in National Solar Vehicle Challenge (NSVC) 2016-17.

We are now working on two prototypes for this academic year 2017-18. We are reaching the final stage of design. The two prototypes are as follows:

Invictus v2.0: The second version of Invictus v1.0, designed to do the same things, but in a much more efficient and reliable way. It is being designed to compete in Electric Solar Vehicle Challenge 2017-18.

Passenger Car (To be named soon): A vehicle capable of carrying around 1 driver and 2-3 passengers around college campus.
