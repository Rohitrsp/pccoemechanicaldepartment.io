Team Red Baron

About us

Team Red Baron is a group of passionate students from Mechanical Engineering Department who are involved in design and development All Terrain Vehicle and compete in various ATV competitions on National and International Platform like BAJASAEINDIA, SAEBAJA International, Enduro Student India etc.

The objective of this activity is to create passion among students about design and development of off-road vehicles and enhance the necessary technical skill sets. The students learn to work as a team to design, build, test, promote and compete a vehicle within the limits of the rules, also to generate financial support for their project simultaneously managing their academic priorities.

Prof. Ummid I. Shaikh
Faculty Coordinator

This is one of the important activities in Mechanical Engineering Department wherein Project-based learning is implemented and promoted.

In addition to refining technical competency, various skills like leadership quality, interpersonal skills, managerial skills, communication skills are also enhanced through this activity making the students more employable, especially for Automobile industry . This activity plays a vital role in overall development of the student and in creating an engineer ready to work in industry.
